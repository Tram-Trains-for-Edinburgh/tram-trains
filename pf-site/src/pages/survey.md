<div class="hero">
  <h1>Have your say on restoring passenger services on the South Sub</h1>
  <p>Your response shapes the campaign to restore passenger services to Edinburgh's South Suburban Railway.</p>
</div>

The South Sub is the freight-only railway curving through south Edinburgh from Gorgie to Portobello. It last carried passengers in 1962. Restoring passenger services using tram-train technology would link the South Sub with Edinburgh's existing tram network, opening up fast, direct journeys across the City of Edinburgh and the Lothians.

We're asking anyone with a view whether you live nearby, travel across the city, or just care about Edinburgh's transport future to tell us how you get around today, which stops you'd use, and what matters most to you. Your answers help us make the case to councillors, MSPs and Scottish Government.

The survey takes about five minutes. Your response is anonymous unless you choose to share your email.
